Learn how to buy, sell, and manage your prediction market positions.

## Trading Guide

Trading on True Market is simple and intuitive. Here's everything you need to know.

## Understanding Market Prices

In prediction markets, share prices represent the market's probability estimate:
- **Price 0.75** = 75% probability this outcome will occur
- **Price 0.25** = 25% probability this outcome will occur
- **All outcomes always sum to $1.00**

## Buying Shares

**Step 1:** Browse markets and select one you're interested in

**Step 2:** Choose the outcome you want to buy shares for

**Step 3:** Enter the amount of MOCA tokens you want to spend

**Step 4:** Preview the number of shares you'll receive

**Step 5:** Click "Buy Shares"

**Step 6:** Approve transaction in your wallet

**Step 7:** Shares are credited to your account after confirmation

## Selling Shares

**Step 1:** Navigate to your portfolio

**Step 2:** Select the position you want to sell

**Step 3:** Enter the number of shares to sell

**Step 4:** Preview the MOCA tokens you'll receive

**Step 5:** Click "Sell Shares"

**Step 6:** Approve transaction in your wallet

**Step 7:** MOCA is sent to your wallet after confirmation

## Order Types

### Market Orders
Buy or sell immediately at current prices. Best for quick execution.

### Limit Orders
Set a specific price you're willing to buy or sell at. Order executes when price reaches your limit.

## Position Management

- **View All Positions** - See all your active market positions
- **Monitor P&L** - Track profit/loss on each position
- **Set Alerts** - Get notified when prices hit certain levels
- **Export History** - Download CSV of your trading activity

## After Market Resolution

When a market resolves:
1. Winning shares automatically become worth $1.00
2. Losing shares become worth $0.00
3. Click "Redeem" on your position
4. Approve transaction in wallet
5. Winnings appear in your wallet
