Track, analyze, and manage all your prediction market positions in one place.

## Portfolio Management

The True Market portfolio dashboard gives you complete visibility into all your positions and performance.

## Portfolio Overview

Your portfolio shows:
- **Total Value** - Current worth of all positions
- **Total Invested** - Total deployed
- **Unrealized P&L** - Profit/loss on open positions
- **Realized P&L** - Profit/loss from closed positions
- **Number of Positions** - Active market positions

## Position Details

For each position, you can see:
- **Market Name** - Which prediction market
- **Outcome** - The outcome you're betting on
- **Quantity** - Number of shares owned
- **Current Price** - Current market price per share
- **Entry Price** - Price you paid per share
- **Position Value** - Current worth of position
- **Unrealized P&L** - Profit/loss if you closed now

## Portfolio Analysis

- **Performance Charts** - Visualize your P&L over time
- **Category Breakdown** - See allocation across market categories
- **Risk Analysis** - Assess your portfolio risk
- **Win Rate** - Percentage of profitable positions
- **ROI by Category** - Return on investment by market type

## Position Actions

From your portfolio, you can:
- **Buy More** - Add to any position
- **Sell** - Close or reduce a position
- **Set Alerts** - Get notified on price changes
- **Share Position** - Share your trades on social media
- **Export Data** - Download position history as CSV
