Protect your assets and account with these essential security practices.

## Security Best Practices

Your security is paramount. Follow these practices to keep your account and assets safe.

## Wallet Security

- **Never Share Your Private Key** - Your private key grants full access to your wallet
- **Secure Your Seed Phrase** - Store safely offline in multiple secure locations
- **Use Hardware Wallets** - Consider using hardware wallets for large amounts
- **Enable Wallet Locking** - Always lock your wallet when away from computer

## Transaction Safety

- **Verify Addresses** - Always verify recipient addresses before sending
- **Check Gas Fees** - Unusual gas fees may indicate a phishing attempt
- **Confirm Market Details** - Verify market details before trading
- **Double-Check Amounts** - Verify amount and outcome before confirming

## Account Protection

- **Strong Passwords** - Use unique, strong passwords everywhere
- **Secure Device** - Keep your device secure with updated software
- **Backup Recovery** - Store recovery information securely offline
- **Monitor Activity** - Regularly check your trading activity and balances

## Risk Management

- **Diversify** - Don't put all funds in one market
- **Start Small** - Begin with small positions to understand the platform
- **Set Limits** - Only trade with money you can afford to lose
- **Use Alerts** - Set price alerts to manage risk on positions

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ Don't click suspicious links in emails or chats
- ❌ Don't download files from untrusted sources
- ❌ Don't enable extensions you don't recognize
- ❌ Don't share your wallet address publicly
- ❌ Don't trust unsolicited investment advice
- ❌ Don't approve unlimited token spending
- ❌ Don't give your seed phrase to anyone

## Common Scams to Avoid

- **Fake Websites** - Always verify URL is correct
- **Phishing Links** - Hover over links before clicking
- **Impersonators** - Real team won't ask for private keys
- **Giveaway Scams** - There's no "free tokens" giveaway
- **Rug Pulls** - Be cautious of new markets with suspicious creators
