## Common Issues

### "Connection Failed" Error

**Problem:** Can't connect wallet to True Market

**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page and try again
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Ensure your wallet extension is enabled
- Check that MOCA network is added to your wallet

### Transaction Pending for Too Long

**Problem:** Your transaction is stuck in pending state

**Solutions:**
- Wait 2-5 minutes for block confirmation
- Check transaction status on block explorer
- If still pending after 15 minutes, you may need to increase gas fee
- Contact support with transaction hash if unresolved

### "Insufficient Balance" Error

**Problem:** "Insufficient balance" error when trying to trade

**Solutions:**
- Ensure wallet is fully synced
- Account for gas fees in your balance
- Wait for pending transactions to confirm
- Deposit more tokens to your wallet

### Market Not Showing

**Problem:** Market doesn't appear in the list

**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page
- Check if the market has been resolved
- Try searching for the market by name or ID
- Clear browser cache and restart

### Gas Fees Too High

**Problem:** Gas fees are higher than expected

**Solutions:**
- Gas fees depend on network congestion
- Try again during off-peak hours
- Use limit orders to reduce rejections
- Check current gas price on the network

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do prediction markets work?

Prediction markets let you buy and sell shares representing different outcomes of future events. Share prices reflect the market's probability estimate. When the event occurs, winning shares are worth $1, while losing shares become worthless.

### What are the fees?

True Market charges:
- **Trading Fee:** 2% on each trade (platform fee)
- **Creator Fee:** 0-5% (set by market creator)
- **Gas Fee:** Depends on network congestion

### Can I trade 24/7?

Yes! True Market operates 24/7. Markets only close when they resolve.

### What if I think a market was resolved incorrectly?

Contact support immediately with evidence. For oracle-resolved markets, disputes go to oracle providers.

### How long do markets stay open?

Markets stay open until their resolution date. The creator sets this date when creating the market.

### Can I cancel a transaction?

Once a transaction is submitted to the blockchain, it cannot be canceled. However, if pending for a long time, it may eventually fail and be rolled back.

### Is True Market safe?

True Market smart contracts are audited. Your funds are only at risk from:
- Incorrect market outcomes (prediction risk)
- Market creator fraud (for manual settlement markets)
- Smart contract vulnerabilities (all contracts audited)

### What's the minimum to start trading?

You need at least 0.5 MOCA tokens to start trading (plus gas fees). Most markets have minimum position sizes of 0.1-1 MOCA.

### Do I need KYC to trade?

No. True Market is fully permissionless and does not require identity verification.

### How do I get tokens?

1. Acquire from exchanges
2. Receive from airdrops
3. Use faucets (if available)
4. Earn from liquidity providing

### Can I withdraw to exchanges?

Yes, you can send your tokens from your wallet to any exchange that supports MOCA deposits.
