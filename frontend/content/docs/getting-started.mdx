## Getting Started

Welcome to True Market! Getting started is simple and takes less than a minute.

## Connecting Your Wallet

Connecting your Stacks wallet is the first step to using True Market.

**Step 1: Visit True Market:** Navigate to truemarket website.

**Step 2: Click Connect Wallet**
You'll be prompted to connect your Web3 wallet

**Step 3: Approve Connection**
Review the permissions and approve the connection in your wallet interface

**Step 4: Add MOCA Network**
If your wallet doesn't have MOCA network configured, add it:
- Chain ID: (MOCA chain ID)
- RPC URL: (MOCA RPC endpoint)
- Currency Symbol: MOCA
- Block Explorer: (MOCA explorer URL)

**That's It!** Once connected, your wallet is ready to use. You'll see your balance and can start exploring markets.

## Getting MOCA Tokens

To start trading, you need MOCA tokens:

1. Acquire MOCA from exchanges or other sources
2. Transfer to your wallet address
3. Wait for transaction confirmation
4. Your balance will appear in True Market dashboard

## Your First Market Position

Once your wallet is connected:

1. **Browse Markets** - Explore active prediction markets
2. **Buy Shares** - Purchase shares for outcomes you believe will happen
3. **Monitor Prices** - Watch how prices change as new information emerges
4. **Trade or Hold** - Sell early or hold until market resolution
5. **Claim Winnings** - When markets resolve, claim your rewards
