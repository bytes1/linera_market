## TrueBot

True Market comes with a built-in AI assistant, **TrueBot**, to help you analyze prediction markets and understand trends. Powered by Google Gemini, TrueBot can help you navigate markets and discover trading opportunities.

## How to Use TrueBot

You can open the TrueBot chat interface from the floating icon at the bottom-right of your dashboard. Simply type your question and TrueBot will do its best to help you.

## What Can TrueBot Do?

TrueBot is designed to be your intelligent prediction market companion. Here are some things you can ask it to do:

### Example Commands

- "What markets are trending today?"
- "Analyze the probability of Bitcoin reaching $120K"
- "Show me my top performing positions"
- "What's the sentiment on this market?"
- "Explain this market to me"
- "Which low-probability outcomes have potential?"
- "Compare outcomes across similar markets"
- "What's my portfolio risk level?"

TrueBot can fetch real-time market data, analyze historical trends, calculate expected value, and help you discover new trading opportunities.

## TrueBot Capabilities

### Market Analysis
Get detailed analysis of any prediction market, including historical prices and volume trends.

### Price Predictions
Receive AI-powered probability estimates and market forecasts.

### Portfolio Insights
Analyze your positions and receive optimization suggestions.

### Real-Time Data
Access current prices, volumes, and market activity.

### Natural Language Processing
Ask questions in plain English and get intelligent responses.
