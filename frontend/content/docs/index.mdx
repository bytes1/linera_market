Welcome to **True Market**, a decentralized prediction market platform built on the **Linera** blockchain.

## Introduction

True Market is a modern, open, and intelligent prediction market platform leveraging Linera's microchain architecture. It provides a comprehensive suite of tools for creating markets, trading prediction shares, and engaging with decentralized forecasting.

**Permissionless & Scalable**

True Market uses Linera's multi-chain protocols to ensure permissionless market creation and infinite scalability. Unlike traditional blockchains where all trades compete for the same block space, Linera allows True Market to run on its own application microchains, ensuring low latency and high throughput.

### Permissionless Markets
Create prediction markets on any topic without approval or permission from centralized authorities.

### Automated Liquidity (AMM)
Built-in **Automated Market Maker (AMM)** ensures constant liquidity for all markets. You trade directly against the smart contract's liquidity pool, enabling instant execution without waiting for a counterparty.

### AI-Powered Insights
Get intelligent market analysis and predictions using our built-in AI assistant, **TrueBot** (powered by Google Gemini).

### Real-Time Trading
Trade prediction shares in real-time. Linera's "Fast Pay" interaction model allows for near-instant settlement of positions.

### Decentralized Settlement
Smart contract-based resolution ensures fair and transparent settlement of all prediction markets.

### Portfolio Management
Track your positions, analyze performance, and manage your prediction market portfolio.

## Our Tech Stack

True Market is built with a modern tech stack to ensure security, scalability, and seamless user experience:

**Frontend:** Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui

**Blockchain:** Linera Protocol (Rust/Wasm)

**Smart Contracts:** Rust (Linera SDK)

**Wallet Integration:** Linera Client / Linera Wallet

**AI & Analytics:** Google Gemini, Real-time Data Processing
